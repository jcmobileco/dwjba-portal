Leadership & Governance

Bi-jurisdictional oversight ensuring transparent, accountable management of Detroit's most vital public facilities.

Executive Leadership

Clarinda Barnett-Harrison

Chief Executive Officer

“

Detroit's civic infrastructure is the backbone of government service — and we take that responsibility seriously.

Clarinda Barnett-Harrison has served as CEO of the Detroit-Wayne Joint Building Authority for over 21 years, making her one of the longest-serving executives in the organization's history. As the first woman to lead the DWJBA, she has overseen landmark capital projects, modernized facility operations, and expanded the Authority's commitment to transparency and public accountability. A proud Detroit native, she brings a deep personal connection to the city she serves.

Board of Commissioners

Governing Structure

The Authority is governed by a joint Board of Commissioners with appointments shared between the City of Detroit and Wayne County. This bi-jurisdictional structure ensures that both governments maintain oversight of public facilities.

Board Chair

Sharon Madison

Madison Madison International

Board Chair Sharon Madison brings decades of leadership experience, having overseen more than $5 billion in development programs. She provides strategic direction and ensures the Authority fulfills its statutory obligations to both the City and County.
