Detroit-Wayne Joint Building Authority

Since 1924, we have been stewards of Detroit's most critical civic infrastructure. The Coleman A. Young Municipal Center stands as a testament to our commitment to excellence, serving the City of Detroit and Wayne County government operations.

We manage facilities that house essential services including executive and legislative branches, judicial operations, and public services. Through meticulous facility management and unwavering dedication to public service, we create environments where Detroit's government and community thrive.

Two Premier Civic Facilities

At the heart of Detroit's civic operations stand two extraordinary facilities, each playing a critical role in serving the City and Wayne County. These are more than buildings. They are anchors of public service, innovation, and community trust.

The Coleman A. Young Municipal Center, housed in one of the region's most architecturally significant structures, serves as the primary government facility for the City of Detroit and Wayne County. It accommodates essential operations including executive, legislative, and judicial functions, while providing critical public services to thousands of residents daily.

The Spirit of Detroit Plaza complements our civic portfolio, offering a historic landmark and vibrant public space. This iconic plaza surrounding the legendary Spirit of Detroit statue creates a gathering place for civic events, celebrations, and community engagement.

Our Authority

Stewards of Detroit's Most Critical Public Buildings

The Detroit-Wayne Joint Building Authority is the bi-jurisdictional body responsible for managing, maintaining, and modernizing the civic infrastructure shared by the City of Detroit and Wayne County. We operate under a mandate of excellence — serving the government agencies, courts, and members of the public who rely on these buildings every day.

From 24/7 security and facilities management to capital improvement programs and sustainability initiatives, the DWJBA operates at the highest standards of public accountability.
