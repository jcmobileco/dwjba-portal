The Technology

Built for Detroit. Designed for Citizens.

Ask CAYMC is a purpose-built AI companion trained specifically on the Coleman A. Young Municipal Center — not a generic chatbot.

Purpose-Built Knowledge Base

Unlike generic AI assistants, Ask CAYMC has been trained with comprehensive, verified information about every department, office, procedure, and service in the building — with real phone numbers, accurate floor locations, and current hours.

Spatial Floor Intelligence

The Interactive Floor Navigator uses spatial reasoning to interpret plain-language requests and translate them into precise physical locations — showing you exactly which floor and office to visit, not just telling you.

Government-Grade Accuracy

Every response is grounded in verified DWJBA data. The system is designed for government use — prioritizing accuracy, clarity, and citizen trust above all else. Sensitive matters are always referred to human staff.
