The Technology

Built for Detroit. Designed for Citizens.

Ask CAYMC is a purpose-built AI companion trained specifically on the Coleman A. Young Municipal Center — not a generic chatbot.

Purpose-Built Knowledge Base

Unlike generic AI assistants, Ask CAYMC is trained with comprehensive, verified information about every department, office, procedure, and service — with real phone numbers, accurate floor locations, and current hours.

Cinematic 3D Navigation

The 3D isometric tower visualizes the entire building in real time. Search your need and watch the exact floor slab illuminate, separate from the stack, and deliver a precise navigation card — no map reading required.

Government-Grade Accuracy

Every response is grounded in verified DWJBA data. The system is designed for government use — prioritizing accuracy, clarity, and citizen trust above all else. Sensitive matters are always referred to human staff.
